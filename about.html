Our Story

Our Beginning

Founded in 2018, Wana Trading started as a small mobile phone shop with a big vision. We wanted to be more than just a retailer - we wanted to be a technology partner for our community.

Growth & Expansion

Through dedication and customer trust, we expanded our services to include audio equipment, printing services, financial services, and visa assistance. Today, we're a comprehensive technology hub.

Our Future

We continue to innovate and adapt to the ever-changing technology landscape. Our commitment remains the same: providing quality products and exceptional service to our valued customers.
