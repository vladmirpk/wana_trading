Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, bank transfers, mobile money (Ecocash, Omari), and major credit cards. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger purchases.
Do you offer warranty on your products?
Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranty. We also provide additional warranty options and excellent after-sales support.
Can you deliver products to my location?
We offer delivery services within Harare and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on location and order value. Contact us for specific rates.
How long does visa processing take?
Processing times vary by country and visa type. Generally, it takes 2-4 weeks for standard visas. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during consultation.
Do you provide technical support?
Yes, we provide comprehensive technical support for all our products. Our team of certified technicians is available to help with setup, troubleshooting, and repairs.
